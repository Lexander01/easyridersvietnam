4 Days, 3 Nights

Dalat ↔ Ho Chi Minh City

Four days between Vietnam's mountain retreat and its buzzing southern metropolis. This route runs in either direction — Dalat to Saigon or Saigon to Dalat. Pickups in Ho Chi Minh City require at least 1 day advance notice.

Tour Itinerary

Shown Dalat → Saigon. The route runs identically in reverse.

Day 1: Dalat – Departure into the Highlands

We leave Dalat and head south through the highlands, passing waterfalls, coffee farms, and jungle-clad hills. The first day is about immersing in the landscape before the urban world arrives.

Day 2: Through the Foothills

The terrain flattens gradually as we descend. We pass through small market towns, rubber plantations, and rural communities that see almost no foreign visitors. Overnight in the foothills.

Day 3: Into the Southern Lowlands

The final highland roads give way to the flat southern plains. The riding is easy and fast, with plenty of stops at local eateries and roadside sights. We overnight close to the outskirts of the city.

Day 4: Arrival in Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)

We ride into Saigon — the city hits you all at once with its energy, traffic, and noise. Your guide drops you at your hotel in the city centre. If starting from Saigon, please book at least 1 day in advance for pickup coordination.

Pricing: 280 EUR / Person

70 EUR/person/day × 4 days. Includes guide, motorcycle, fuel, 3 nights accommodation, and insurance. Pickups in Ho Chi Minh City require at least 1 day advance notice.
