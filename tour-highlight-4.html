5 Days, 4 Nights

Hoi An to Dalat

The Ho Chi Minh Trail in reverse — from the ancient streets of Hoi An, south through the Central Highlands and down into Dalat's mountain cool. At least 1–2 days advance notice required for pickup.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Hoi An – Pickup & Departure

Your guide meets you in Hoi An (minimum 1–2 days advance notice required for pickup coordination). We head south, leaving the coastal plains and starting the climb into the hills.

Day 2: Into the Highlands

The road climbs deeper into the Central Highlands. We follow the Ho Chi Minh Trail south — the same route as Highlight 1 in reverse — through wartime history, dense jungle, and remote villages.

Day 3: Mountain Passes

A day of high-altitude riding on dramatic switchbacks with sweeping panoramas. Your guide narrates the history of the trail and the region's minority cultures at key stops.

Day 4: Deep Highlands

We continue south through increasingly remote terrain — coffee plantations, pine forests, and isolated communities rarely visited by outsiders. Overnight in the highlands.

Day 5: Arrival in Dalat

The final day brings us into Dalat — Vietnam's city of eternal spring. We arrive through winding mountain roads and flower farms, dropping you at your hotel in the cool highland air.

Pricing: 350 EUR / Person

70 EUR/person/day × 5 days. Includes guide, motorcycle, fuel, 4 nights accommodation, and insurance. Please book at least 1–2 days in advance for Hoi An pickup.
