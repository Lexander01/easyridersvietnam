Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a motorcycle license?

If you ride with our guides, you do not need a license. If you choose the self-drive option, you must have a valid international driving permit that covers motorcycles.

What happens with my heavy luggage?

Our motorcycles are equipped with sturdy racks. We securely tie down your backpacks or suitcases in waterproof bags before we hit the road. It travels with you.

What if it rains?

We provide high-quality rain gear (jackets, pants, and boot covers). If the weather becomes dangerous, we will pause the tour or reroute. Safety is our priority.

Are meals included?

Breakfast and drinks are usually included at our homestays. Lunch and dinner are not included in the base price, allowing you the flexibility to choose what you want to eat. We will take you to excellent local spots.
