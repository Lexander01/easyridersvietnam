One-Day Local Dalat Tour

The best way to discover Dalat in a single day — a local guide, a motorbike, and the hidden corners of the city and its surrounding countryside that most tourists never find.

What to Expect

Pickup from your hotel in Dalat — return drop-off by early evening.

Morning: Countryside & Coffee

We start early and head out of the city into the surrounding farmland — flower fields, strawberry farms, coffee plantations, and vegetable gardens. We stop for a Vietnamese coffee at a local spot with a view that no guidebook will tell you about.

Midday: Hidden Spots & Local Food

Your guide takes you to local lunch spots and lesser-known attractions around Dalat — places not on the tourist map. This is where the real Dalat is found. Expect excellent Vietnamese food and genuine local atmosphere.

Afternoon: Waterfalls, Valleys & Viewpoints

The afternoon takes in scenic viewpoints, a waterfall or two, and a final ride through pine forests back to the city as the light turns golden. Drop-off at your accommodation by early evening.

Your guide tailors the day to your interests — exact stops may vary. The map shows typical areas visited around Dalat.
