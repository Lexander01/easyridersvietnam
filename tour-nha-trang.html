2 Days, 1 Night

Highlands to the Coast

Drop from the cool mountains of Dalat down to the tropical beaches of Nha Trang.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Dalat to Lak Lake

Travel through isolated mountain roads. We stop at local farms and minority villages, giving you a raw look at rural Vietnamese life. We end the day at Lak Lake, sleeping in a traditional M'Nong longhouse.

Day 2: Lak Lake to Nha Trang

After a morning canoe ride on the lake, we hit the spectacular Phoenix Pass. The landscape shifts dramatically from dense jungle to coastal plains as we descend into Nha Trang, dropping you off at your hotel.

Pricing: 140 EUR / Person

Includes guide, motorcycle, fuel, 1 night accommodation, and insurance.
