The Guide Centre

Riders First, Guides Second

We should be upfront: we are not the guides. We are two students from the Netherlands who came to Vietnam, took one of these tours, and were so blown away that we wanted to help. The guides told us their bookings had collapsed since the Ha Giang Loop went viral — so we built this platform to do their marketing.

The guide centre is based in Dalat. Most of the guides were born within a few hundred kilometres of the roads they ride. They grew up on these passes, spent their childhoods in the villages along the Ho Chi Minh Trail, and know every curve, every guesthouse, and every roadside pho spot between here and Hoi An.

They speak fluent English and Vietnamese, and have real relationships with local minority communities along all of their routes. When your guide stops at a village and is greeted by name, that is not a performance — it is thirty years of friendship.

Groups stay small — a maximum of ten riders — because that is how the guides have always worked. Not a convoy, not a package tour. Your guide is not following a script. They are sharing their home with you.

Every guide holds a Vietnamese motorcycle guide licence and carries full emergency training. Your safety is the foundation on which every tour is built.

Our Standards

What We Expect from Every Guide

🗺️

Deep Local Knowledge

Every guide must know their route well enough to divert, adapt, and discover — not just follow a GPS. We test this before anyone rides with a customer.

🤝

Community Relationships

Our guides have real friendships with the families along our routes. This opens doors — and guesthouses — that no guidebook could ever find.

🛡️

Safety First, Always

Licensed, trained, and experienced. Our guides read the road constantly — pace, weather, group energy — and adjust without you ever needing to ask.

💬

Real Communication

Fluent English, genuine humour, and the patience to answer the same question twice. A great guide makes you feel at home from the first kilometre.
