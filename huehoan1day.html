Hue to Hoi An

From Vietnam's imperial capital to the ancient lantern-lit streets of Hoi An — crossing the legendary Hai Van Pass and the dramatic Da Nang coastline in a single unforgettable day.

⏰ Please book at least 2 days in advance. Our guides are based in Dalat and need time to travel to Hue before the tour starts.

What to Expect

Your guide meets you at your hotel in Hue and drops you off in Hoi An by late afternoon.

Morning: Hue to the Hai Van Pass

We leave Hue early and ride south through Lang Co — a narrow peninsula of lagoon and sea — before beginning the climb up the Hai Van Pass. The road rises steeply through jungle into the clouds, with views back over the Lang Co lagoon that will stop you in your tracks. At the summit we pass old wartime fortifications and look south toward Da Nang bay.

Midday: Descent to Da Nang

The descent from the pass is one of the great motorcycle moments in Vietnam — sweeping curves, jungle on both sides, and the entire Da Nang coastline opening up below. We ride through Da Nang and stop at the Marble Mountains if you'd like to climb for views over the sea.

Afternoon: Arrival in Hoi An

The final stretch south takes us through coconut groves and coastal villages into Hoi An. We drop you at your hotel in or near the Ancient Town in the late afternoon — giving you the evening to explore the famous lantern-lit streets at their most beautiful.
