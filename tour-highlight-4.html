5 Days · 4 Nights

Hoi An to Dalat

The Ho Chi Minh Trail in reverse — from the ancient lantern-lit streets of Hoi An, south through the Central Highlands and into Dalat's cool mountain air. A journey from coast to highlands.

⏰ Please book at least 2 days in advance. Our guides are based in Dalat and need time to travel to Hoi An before the tour starts. Same-day or next-day bookings cannot be accepted for this route.

Tour Itinerary

Starting in Hoi An — your guide meets you at your hotel.

Day 1: Hoi An — Pickup & Departure

Your guide meets you in Hoi An. We leave the ancient town behind and head south, leaving the coastal plains and starting the climb into the hills. The landscape changes quickly — from rice paddies to dense jungle.

Day 2: Into the Highlands

The road climbs deeper into the Central Highlands. We follow the Ho Chi Minh Trail south through wartime history, dense jungle, and remote villages. Overnight in a guesthouse deep in the highlands.

Day 3: Mountain Passes

A day of high-altitude riding on dramatic switchbacks with sweeping panoramas. Your guide narrates the history of the trail and the region's minority cultures at key stops along the way.

Day 4: Deep Highlands

We continue south through increasingly remote terrain — coffee plantations, pine forests, and isolated communities rarely visited by outsiders. The air cools further as we gain altitude. Overnight in the highlands.

Day 5: Arrival in Dalat

The final day brings us into Dalat — Vietnam's city of eternal spring. We arrive through winding mountain roads and flower farms, dropping you at your hotel in the cool highland air.
