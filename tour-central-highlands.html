3 Days, 2 Nights

The Ultimate Highlands Loop

Dalat to Buon Ma Thuot. Ride the legendary Ho Chi Minh trail untouched by mass tourism.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Dalat to Lak Lake

Ride through vegetable and flower farms. Descend into the jungle via twisting mountain passes. We stay overnight in a traditional longhouse next to Lak Lake.

Day 2: Lak Lake to Buon Ma Thuot

Explore the local M'Nong villages. Ride dirt tracks through rubber and pepper plantations. We end the day in the coffee capital of Vietnam, Buon Ma Thuot.

Day 3: The Ho Chi Minh Trail

Visit the spectacular Dray Nur and Dray Sap waterfalls. Ride a section of the historic Ho Chi Minh trail before finishing the loop back in Dalat or continuing your journey onward.

Pricing: 210 EUR / Person

Includes guide, motorcycle, fuel, 2 nights accommodation, entrance fees, and insurance.
