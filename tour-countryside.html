1 Day Tour

Dalat Countryside Loop

Waterfalls, coffee plantations, and minority villages just outside the city limits.

Tour Itinerary

Full Day: Dalat and Surrounds

We pick you up at your hotel at 8:30 AM. Leaving the city, we ride through winding pine forest roads to visit a local flower farm, a weasel coffee plantation, and a silk factory. After a local lunch, we hike down to Elephant Waterfall and visit the massive Linh An Pagoda before returning to Dalat by 5:00 PM.

Pricing: 40 EUR / Person

Includes guide, motorcycle, fuel, entrance fees, and insurance.
