5 Days · 4 Nights

Dalat to Hoi An

Follow the legendary Ho Chi Minh Trail north through the Central Highlands — dramatic mountain passes, remote minority villages, and ancient history leading to the lantern-lit streets of Hoi An.

Tour Itinerary

Starting in Dalat — your guide picks you up at your hotel.

Day 1: Dalat — Departure into the Highlands

We leave Dalat's cool mountain air and head north, winding through pine forests and flower farms. First stops include local villages and roadside coffee stops with views that open onto the highland plateau. Overnight in a guesthouse in the highlands.

Day 2: Deep into the Highlands

We continue along the Ho Chi Minh Trail, passing through remote jungle and stepping into communities that have seen very few foreign visitors. Dense forest, wartime history, and highland guesthouses that feel a world away. Overnight deep in the highlands.

Day 3: Mountain Passes and History

A day of dramatic riding — steep switchbacks, sweeping valley views, and wartime landmarks along the old Ho Chi Minh Trail. Your guide brings the history to life at key stopping points.

Day 4: Coast Descent

The landscape shifts as we descend toward the coast. The air warms, the jungle thins, and the South China Sea appears on the horizon. Tonight we stay near the coast.

Day 5: Arrival in Hoi An

The final push into Hoi An — through paddy fields and fishing villages. We drop you at your accommodation in the Ancient Town. Your adventure ends here, but the memories ride on.
