4 Days · 3 Nights

Ho Chi Minh City to Dalat

Four days riding from Saigon's chaotic energy up through the southern lowlands, into the highlands, and finally to Dalat — Vietnam's cool, misty mountain city. A journey from heat and noise to highland serenity.

⏰ Please book at least 2 days in advance. Our guides are based in Dalat and need time to travel to Ho Chi Minh City before the tour starts. Same-day or next-day bookings cannot be accepted for this route.

Tour Itinerary

Starting in Ho Chi Minh City — your guide meets you at your hotel.

Day 1: Ho Chi Minh City — Into the Countryside

Your guide meets you at your hotel in Ho Chi Minh City. We leave the city traffic behind and head north through the flat southern plains. The contrast with urban Saigon is immediate — rubber plantations, market towns, and quiet country roads. Overnight in the foothills.

Day 2: Climbing Through the Foothills

The landscape starts to rise as we enter the foothills of the Central Highlands. We pass through small villages, coffee and tea farms, and dense jungle. The temperature drops noticeably. Overnight in a guesthouse in the highlands.

Day 3: Deep into the Highlands

Full highland riding today — winding mountain roads, viewpoints over jungle valleys, and stops at waterfalls and local villages that see almost no foreign visitors. One of the most scenic days on any route we offer. Overnight close to Dalat.

Day 4: Arrival in Dalat

The final stretch into Dalat. We arrive in the city in the afternoon, with time to explore the market and lake area before your guide drops you at your hotel. Tour complete.
