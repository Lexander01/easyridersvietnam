2 Days, 1 Night

Dalat to Mui Ne Beach

Drop from Dalat's mountain cool down through the highlands and into the desert-like dunes and tropical beaches of Mui Ne — one of Vietnam's most dramatic landscape transitions.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Dalat to the Foothills

We leave Dalat and head southeast, winding down through pine forests and terraced farmland. The temperature climbs as we descend. We stop at local spots along the way and overnight in the foothills.

Day 2: Coastal Desert – Arrival in Mui Ne

The final stretch is unforgettable — the landscape transforms into red and white sand dunes as we approach the coast. We arrive in Mui Ne and drop you at your beachfront hotel, ready for sun, sea, and sand.
