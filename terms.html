2. The Booking Process

When you submit a booking through this website:

  • You pay a 20% deposit online to secure your spot. This deposit is processed through our payment provider and confirms your reservation.
  • We share your booking details with the guide centre and provide you with a WhatsApp contact to finalise your tour arrangements directly with the guides.
  • The remaining 80% of the tour price is paid in cash (Vietnamese Dong, EUR, or USD) directly to your guide on the first day of the tour.

The 20% deposit is strictly and unconditionally non-refundable under all circumstances. There are no exceptions. By completing your booking you explicitly accept this condition.

This means: regardless of the reason for cancellation — including illness, travel disruptions, visa issues, weather, changes of plan, or any other circumstance — the deposit will not be returned. By submitting your booking you confirm that you have read and understood this.

The deposit covers the cost of reserving your spot, coordinating with the guide centre, and the administrative work involved in processing your booking. Once a booking is confirmed, the guide centre allocates a guide and scooter to your tour dates.

If you need to change your tour dates, please contact the guide centre directly via WhatsApp as early as possible. Date changes are subject to availability and are at the guide centre's discretion. The deposit is not transferable to a different booking.

4. The Money-Back Guarantee

The guide centre operates a 100% satisfaction guarantee on the tour experience. This guarantee is provided by the guides directly — not by us as a platform.

In practice this works as follows: the 80% balance you pay in cash to your guide on Day 1 can be refunded in full by your guide, in cash, on the spot, if the experience falls short of what was described. There is no form to fill in and no email to send. You speak to your guide.
