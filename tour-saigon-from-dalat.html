4 Days · 3 Nights

Dalat to Ho Chi Minh City

Four days riding from Vietnam's misty mountain retreat down to its buzzing southern metropolis. Coffee farms, jungle highlands, rubber plantations, and the dramatic contrast as the city finally appears on the horizon.

Tour Itinerary

Starting in Dalat — your guide picks you up at your hotel.

Day 1: Dalat — Departure into the Highlands

We leave Dalat in the morning and head south through the highlands, passing waterfalls, coffee farms, and jungle-clad hills. The first day is about immersing in the landscape before the urban world arrives. Overnight in a guesthouse in the foothills.

Day 2: Through the Foothills

The terrain flattens gradually as we descend. We pass through small market towns, rubber plantations, and rural communities that see almost no foreign visitors. Plenty of stops at local roadside eateries. Overnight in the foothills.

Day 3: Into the Southern Lowlands

The final highland roads give way to the flat southern plains. The riding is easy and fast, with stops at local sights and a relaxed lunch by a river. Overnight close to the outskirts of Ho Chi Minh City.

Day 4: Arrival in Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)

We ride into Saigon — the city hits you all at once with its energy, traffic, and noise. Your guide drops you at your hotel in the city centre. Tour complete.
