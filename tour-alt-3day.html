3 Days, 2 Nights

Alternative 3-Day Trip

Dalat to Ta Dung Lake, across to Lak Lake, and on to Buon Ma Thuot — then catch a sleeper bus to Hoi An overnight, arriving fresh the next morning.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Dalat to Ta Dung Lake

We depart Dalat and ride west through coffee highlands and jungle trails toward Ta Dung Lake — a stunning reservoir hidden inside a national park. Overnight near the lake.

Day 2: Ta Dung Lake to Lak Lake

A cross-country ride through the heart of the Central Highlands to Lak Lake, the region's largest natural lake. We overnight in a traditional M'Nong longhouse on the lakeshore.

Day 3: Lak Lake to Buon Ma Thuot — then Hoi An by Sleeper Bus

We ride from Lak Lake to Buon Ma Thuot (BMT) city — the coffee capital of Vietnam. In the evening, you board a sleeper bus to Hoi An, arriving the following morning refreshed and ready to explore the Ancient Town.

Pricing: 210 EUR / Person

70 EUR/person/day × 3 days. Includes guide, motorcycle, fuel, 2 nights accommodation, and insurance. Sleeper bus to Hoi An not included.
